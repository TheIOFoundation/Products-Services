**FAQ: Answers to Your Questions**

**Header:** Your Questions, Answered

**Q: What types of events do you manage?**\
A: We handle fundraisers, conferences, galas, workshops, virtual/hybrid events, and more for NGOs and organizations.

**Q: Can you work with limited budgets?**\
A: Yes, we tailor plans to fit any budget while ensuring high-impact results.

**Q: Do you support virtual events?**\
A: Absolutely, we offer advanced virtual and hybrid event solutions for global reach.

**Q: How do your fiscal hosting services integrate?**\
A: Our fiscal hosting ensures seamless management of event funds, from donations to sponsorships, with full transparency.

**Q: How far in advance should we plan?**\
A: We recommend 3–6 months for in-person events and 1–3 months for virtual events, but we can work with tighter timelines.
