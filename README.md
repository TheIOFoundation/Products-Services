# Introduction

## About

At The IO Foundation, we have developed a line of Products and Services that is designed to support NGOs and Open Source projects with effective, streamlined solutions that align with our Data-Centric Digital Rights (DCDR) advocacy. Through our offerings, we enable organizations in achieving their missions while promoting independence from external dependencies and constraints, fostering a resilient and autonomous nonprofit sector.
